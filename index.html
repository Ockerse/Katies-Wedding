Q + A

WHAT SHOULD I WEAR?
The dress code for our wedding is semi-formal to dressy casual! Dresses, jumpsuits, regular suits, dress shirts, etc. The wedding party will be primarily in various shades of light blues and navy while the florals will be a variety of different, spring-like colors!

WHAT WILL THE WEATHER BE LIKE THIS TIME OF YEAR?
April can be a bit unpredictable, but expect 65-70s. Rain (or even snow!) could happen, but the ceremony and reception will be indoors!

WHAT TIME SHOULD I ARRIVE?
Guests should arrive 30 minutes to an hour before the start of the ceremony to make sure everyone is on time!
ARE KIDS ALLOWED?
Yes! Kids are allowed. If needed, servings of mac and cheese can be made available for any children.

WHAT HAPPENS AFTER THE CEREMONY?
There will be a cocktail hour following the ceremony! Guests can head straight to Venue3Two after the ceremony concludes for finger food and beverages

WHERE CAN WE PARK?
There is parking available in the back of the church, or across the street! There is plenty around that is free to use. For the reception, there is a parking lot available at the front of Venue3Two.

CAN I BRING A DATE?
If you'd like to bring a guest that isn't listed on your invite, just ask Austin or Katie and write their name on the RSVP!

FACE MASKS?
We prefer if guests could wear face masks indoors when they aren't seated, but not required especially if vaccinated.
